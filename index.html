Situs Partners (SITUS) is a boutique private equity firm focused on acquisition, conversion and development of real estate. Through the restructure and expansion of our previous company, we have over 17 years of combined experience in the investment and real estate industry and currently have over 10 million dollars in assets under management. SITUS is based out of the New York City metro area and actively monitors the local real estate market in order to recognize opportunities as they arise.


Situs Partners (SITUS) was formed by David Z. Chen and Daniel Tang in 2012.
